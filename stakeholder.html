The Stakeholder

Who are they? Why do they care? Why does it make life easier?

  • Students
    • Students need the best tools at their disposal when it comes to researching the world around them.
  • Teachers/Tutors
    • Being able to make specific recommendations to students and get immediate feedback on the effectiveness of a resource is invaluable to teachers and tutors.
  • Booksellers/E-book Stores
    • Booksellers/E-book stores could be losing potential customers and profit that they would otherwise get if it was simpler and easier for readers to know which books to get.
  • Libraries
    • More people would sustain their library memberships, as they would continually have books recommended towards them that fit their liking.
