What is AskMissy?

AskMissy is a personalized resource finder designed to make your life easier. Using Predictive Algorithms, our software will learn just what the best resources are for your specific needs. AskMissy is targeted towards students, teachers, and librarians in grades 6-12 to make finding research materials easier.
