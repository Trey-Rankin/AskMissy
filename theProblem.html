The Problem

Students are suffering from a lack of personalized study material.

Over the COVID-19 pandemic student SOL Score have dropped across the board, and this is evident in math and english scores.

How are they Suffering?

  • Students find it difficult to find resouces that work well with thier learning style
  • Students have too many options when it comes to finding resources - there needs to be a way of narrowing down resources to fit the needs of the student. (specifically designed around you and your personal preferences)

Why are they Suffering?

  • Some students may not have good teachers and tutors to guide them to find resources
  • Students are not aware of where they can find physical copies of resources
  • Students may not be aware of what kind of learning style they have

What is cost?

An inefficient or outdated resource search engine could lead to inaccurate results or a student may waste time using multiple searches to get a result. If a reader does find a resource but realizes it doesn't fit their learning style or preferences upon already reading it, they may feel unmotivated to learn. Money is a concern to those who buy hardcopy books or E-books where they are nonrefundable. So AskMissy seeks to connect students with existing resources at their local libraries.
